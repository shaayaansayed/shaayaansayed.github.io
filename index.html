Intelligent Urban Policymaking
ExpeditionHacks Grand Prize, AIGrant Finalist
Satellite images, increasing in frequency and resolution, contain valuable information about city processes such as urban sprawl. This project fit a deep vision model to classify cities into urban classes and identify urbanization over time. By pairing this model with social and economic data found in the US census, the end product, a visualization tool catered to policymakers, aims to identify "strained" counties, areas that are growing in population without the proper resources needed to support them.

Homie AI
InternHackATX 2nd Place
Improving customer engagement on HomeAway's browsing interface is important for increasing the likelihood that customers make reservations. This project presents a photo intelligence engine that can classify the various images found in a listing. This model has many uses including organizing listing images thereby enhancing the browsing experience, and also using it in an intelligent chatbot.
